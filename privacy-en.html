testBeat Metronome - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Thank you for using testBeat (the "App"). We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data may be processed when you use the App. Please read it before use.

1. Information We Collect or Use

  • Microphone recording: When you use the "Custom Voice" feature to record count-in beats, the App requests microphone permission. Recording is processed locally on your device to generate beat sounds and is not uploaded to our servers or any third party. You can disable microphone access at any time in your device settings.
  • Local storage: Your recorded custom voice files and app settings (such as tempo, time signature, and haptic feedback preferences) are stored only on your device. We cannot access them.

2. Third-Party Service: Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and app usage information to provide personalized ads and measure ad performance. For details about Google's data collection and use, please see:

Google Privacy Policy

We do not provide your recordings or local app settings to advertising providers. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (for iOS: Settings - Privacy & Security - Tracking/Advertising).

3. What We Do Not Collect

The App does not require registration or login. We do not collect your name, email address, account credentials, or other directly identifiable personal information.

4. Data Security and Storage

Your recordings and settings are stored only on your own device. We do not store, transmit, or back up this data. Please keep your device secure, as uninstalling the App may remove local data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. For material changes, we may also provide notice in the App or by other appropriate means.
